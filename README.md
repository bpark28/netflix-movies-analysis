Metric 1: How fast has Netflix expanded its catalog?
Business Question: How has the Netflix's content catalog grown over time?
Key findings:
    - Growth started accelerating around 2016/2017 where it showed significant increase in numbers of movies/shows added to Netflix.
    - In 2019 Highest number of movies/shows, 2016, were added to Netflix.
    - Since 2017, the number of movies/shows added each year has been fairly steady, average of 1646 added per year.
Business Interpretation:
    - Rapid increase in content additions beginning around 2016/2016 suggests that Netflix was heavily invested in expanding its content library to attract new subscribers and strengthening its competitve position in the streaming market.
    - It's peak in 2019 indicates a period of aggressive content acuisition and production, likely aimed at increasing viewer choice and supporting subscriber growth across different regions and audience segments.
    - The consistant number of titles added each year after 2017 shows that Netflix has established a stable pipeline, allowing the platform to continuously refresh its catalog and retain existing subscribers.
Strategic Implications:
    - Future content strategies may focus on maintaining growth while improving content quality and diversity.
